About Me

Born and raised in North Jersey, I'm a passionate Software Engineer with a strong foundation in full stack development and quality assurance. I graduated from Rutgers University School of Engineering with a BS in Electrical & Computer Engineering and a BA in Computer Science in May 2019. Since then, I have gained extensive experience in developing and optimizing complex software solutions across various industries. Currently, I am working as a Software Engineer at Thrivent, where I spearhead the development of sophisticated financial applications using React.js, HTML, and CSS. My role involves integrating applications with GraphQL and AWS, optimizing test run times through parallelization techniques, and creating robust CI/CD pipelines with GitHub Actions. Previously, I served as a Senior Software Engineer at Routable, where I expanded frontend and backend automation infrastructure, reducing manual regression testing by 60% and improving CI/CD pipeline efficiency. My expertise in Cypress, Pytest, and Postman allowed me to implement effective automated API and E2E feature tests. In addition to my technical roles, I have a proven track record in project management and agile methodologies. My skills include Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, NodeJS, AWS, SQL, Terraform, Kubernetes, Docker, and more. I am adept at leading teams, mentoring junior developers, and continuously improving software development processes.
